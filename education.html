Reality of Education System

Samajhna zaroori hai — tabhi sudhaar possible hai

System ka sach

Education system ne crores logon ko basic knowledge di hai — ye ek strong foundation hai. Lekin aaj ke time me kuch problems bhi hain jinko samajhna zaroori hai. Goal system ko blame karna nahi, balki usko samajhkar apni growth ko better banana hai.

📚 Ratta System

Students ko samajhne se zyada yaad karne par focus karaya jata hai.

🏆 Marks Race

Marks ko success ka main measure bana diya gaya hai.

💼 Skills Gap

Real world skills jaise communication, tech, problem solving kam sikhayi jati hain.

🧠 Creativity Down

Creative thinking aur innovation ko kam importance milta hai.
